Taekwondo Classes in Wembley

Welcome to Lindsay Lawrence Taekwondo, offering professional Taekwondo training in North West and West London, London. We welcome students from Stanmore, Kingston, Wembley, Brent, Harrow and Alperton.

Our classes focus on discipline, confidence, self-defence, fitness and respect. Suitable for beginners and experienced students aged 5 and above.

Meet Your Instructor

Master Donstan Kitt

Master Donstan Kitt is the lead instructor at DK Wembley Taekwondo. He is dedicated to developing confidence, discipline and technical excellence in students of all ages.

He trained under Grandmaster Lindsay Lawrence, gaining deep expertise in traditional Taekwondo, competition training and martial arts leadership.

Welcoming students from Wembley, Brent, Harrow and Alperton, classes are suitable for all levels from age 5+.
